DEFEAT IS A STATE OF MIND

Emancipate yourself from mental slavery ~ Bob Marley

A collective student blog created by Dennis mbugua in Kenya to improve mental health,
Student Minds offers insights, tips and motivation for students on areas like campaigning,
exams, fundraising, graduation, anxiety, discrimination and a load of other topics.
Written by student for students, this blog uses personal experiences to guide others through their college years.
It can also offer tips and tricks for dealing with pressure and mental health issues.
